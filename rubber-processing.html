Our Production Process

At Jeety Rubber, we combine responsible sourcing, modern processing technology, and rigorous quality management to produce high-quality natural rubber for global industries. Our production flow is engineered for consistency, efficiency, and traceability while safeguarding the proprietary methods that give our products their reliability.

Production Capacity (Per Annum) 36,000 Metric Tons
Number of Shifts (Per Day) 3 (Three)
Products TSR 5, TSR 10, TSR 20
Quality Management System ISO 9001:2015 (Certified by SGS)
Environmental Management System ISO 14001:2015 (In Progress)

From Cup Lump to Technically Specified Rubber (TSR)

Ethical Sourcing of Cup Lump

At Jeety Rubber, our production journey begins with the people and communities at the heart of Liberia’s natural rubber sector. We source all of our cup lump from Salala Rubber Corporation (SRC) and trusted local farmholders, paying in accordance with the Liberia National Rubber Pricing Commission’s monthly pricing to ensure fairness and transparency. This values-driven approach creates a strong foundation for the quality and integrity that define our supply chain.

Pre-Processing and Material Preparation

When cup lump arrives at our facility, it undergoes a careful preparation process. The material is cleaned, inspected, and graded to ensure consistency before entering the production line. These early steps are crucial in establishing the uniformity required for dependable natural rubber.

Controlled Processing and Milling

Inside the facility, using modern, energy-efficient machinery, the material moves through a streamlined processing flow that combines modern machinery with disciplined oversight. The material undergoes carefully controlled milling, blending, and drying. Our process flow is designed to preserve product integrity and ensure stable, consistent, high-performance TSR.

Quality Assurance and Laboratory Testing

Quality is reinforced at every stage of our operations. Our on-site laboratory tests each batch against international standards, ensuring that the rubber we produce is consistent, durable, and ready for diverse industrial applications. Only after meeting these rigorous criteria is the material packaged carefully for delivery to customers around the world.

Packaging and Global Distribution

Finished TSR is packaged to protect quality during storage and transport. Our logistics systems ensure safe handling and timely delivery to customers worldwide.

Our Commitment to Standards

Our commitment to excellence is reflected in our certifications. Jeety Rubber is fully certified under ISO 9001, highlighting our dedication to strong quality management systems. We are also in the process of implementing ISO 14001, further affirming our responsibility to environmental stewardship as we grow.

Standards That Reflect Our Values

Warm in tone yet precise in execution, our production process represents the balance of community partnership, technical capability, and rigorous quality control that defines Jeety Rubber.
